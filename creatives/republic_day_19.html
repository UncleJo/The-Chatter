Republic Day

January 26, 2019 Kathyaini Rao

On a cold Saturday morning, the SMIT NCC contingent left for Tadong to attend a parade competition which was a part of Republic Day celebrations taking place at Sikkim Manipal Institute of Medical Sciences. It was indeed a matter of pride for SMIT as this was the first batch of SMIT NCC and the first time that they ever represented their college outside. The celebrations commenced with the arrival of the guest of honor, Air Marshal Joneja, VM, and Dean & Officiating VC of Sikkim Manipal University. The festivities began with a splendidly coordinated march past by five contingents, all from different colleges of the university. Afterward, the chief guest took the stage and addressed the audience present. This got followed by several remarkable cultural performances put together by the students. Subsequently, the award ceremony took place where Sikkim Manipal College of Nursing won top honors, closely followed by Sikkim Manipal College of Physiotherapy and finally, Sikkim Manipal Institute of Technology, who stood third. The distribution of snacks in the hostel mess concluded the passionate celebrations.
