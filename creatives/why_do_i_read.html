Why Do I Read?

January 30, 2019 Aditya Uapdhyay

The reason I read is not that I seek knowledge, I read because I seek escape from this reality that bounds me.

Whenever I hold a book in my hands. I don’t see it as just pieces of printed papers stitched together. What I see is a portal, a gateway to parallel worlds where anything is possible and the only thing that bounds me from exploring these possibilities is my own imagination. A writer just provides us with a skeleton, it’s us who put on flesh and skin on it.

This divine feeling of escaping your body and being transformed into another world; living another life, dreaming another dream or seeing the world from a completely different perspective; while sitting in a corner is what drags me back to a book every time. It provides me with an escape from my mortality; years and lives pass through just in a matter of pages. I dull the world around me and leap into my own realm, Away from all the chaos. I coexist between several worlds leaping from one to another while raiding tombs, slaying beasts and winning wars.
