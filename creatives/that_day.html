That day

So ,it’s all about 16th July ,2018....

January 31st, 2019 Ayushi Sinha

I was over the moon about the fact that I was getting into the college life. No doubt while I was looking forward to the opportunities that were knocking at my door, I was a bit tensed too.

As time got closer and closer for me to leave my home where I had been living since 18 years , the anxiety level kept increasing .My mind was flooded with various thoughts , whether I made the right decision ,will I be able to cope up with the environment and blah blah blah...

And finally the day came , 16th of July ,2018. It was around 23:00,the time to leave my hometown. I was getting dimmer and dimmer ,losing all my courage as I was going miles apart from my family . With the mixture of fear and excitement I finally boarded the train. Seeing my parents worried ,broke me a little. The whistle blew and the train left the station...

All over the way I kept thinking about my family and friends. As soon as I reached closer to my destination, the fear got replaced with excitement. No matter how excited I was about my college, leaving my home kept ripping me apart.

I came across several new faces in the college, had interactions with few of them.

Months passed!!

It’s funny how the faces that once seemed new and unknown are my family now. They are the ones who don’t let me miss my home . I got a new family here in just a time so little .

Little did I know I would be calling it the best phase of my life, from studies to all the various activities ,from games and fun to semester exams. I am gladly going to call it my right decision.

Even those no one can make me not miss or forget my family but these people here surely lessen the pain and grief. With them I m ready to face all the new challenges now and in future too. Let’s see what’s next planned for us!!
